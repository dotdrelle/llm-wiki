# Demo Project Brief

The team is preparing a small internal knowledge base for the Orion support project.

The first goal is to centralize operational notes that are currently scattered across chat messages and shared documents. The expected users are support engineers, service managers, and project stakeholders.

Known requirements:

- support engineers need a quick way to find current procedures;
- service managers need a short weekly status note;
- project stakeholders need traceable decisions and open questions;
- generated notes must cite the source material used by the wiki.

Current open questions:

- the final list of source repositories is not confirmed;
- the owner of the weekly status note is not assigned;
- retention rules for obsolete procedures are still undefined.

The next review meeting is planned for 2026-06-15.
